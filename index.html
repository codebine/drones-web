Our Story

Robotics that Reach the Unreachable

Krishinetra builds advanced drone and robotic systems for safe, precise inspection and deep cleaning of glass façades and solar panels. Our proprietary navigation lets drones access hard-to-reach places quickly, reducing risky manual work and cutting downtime for clients.

  • Inspection & Analysis: High-resolution imaging and automated defect detection.
  • Deep Cleaning: Non-abrasive cleaning solutions for glass and photovoltaic surfaces.
  • Data & Reporting: Actionable reports for maintenance and performance optimisation.
