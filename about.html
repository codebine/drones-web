Our Story

Automation That Protects People & Assets

Krishinetra develops autonomous drone and robotic systems for safe, precise inspection and deep cleaning of glass façades, roofs and solar panels. Our proprietary navigation and analytics replace risky manual access with reliable, fast and data-rich operations — helping clients reduce downtime, lower costs and improve safety.

  • Proprietary Navigation: Precise autonomous flight in complex urban and industrial environments.
  • Sensor Suite: High-resolution imagery, thermal, LiDAR and multispectral sensors for actionable insight.
  • Cleaning Systems: PV-safe, non-abrasive cleaning tailored for glass and solar modules.
  • Reporting: Automated inspection reports and asset-health dashboards for maintenance planning.

Our solutions serve real estate, renewable energy and industrial clients — delivering safer operations and measurable ROI.
