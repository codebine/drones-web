How Krishinetra Works

We combine hardware, autonomy and services to deliver safe and repeatable field operations:

1. Mission Planning

Site survey & risk assessment followed by mission plan generation to keep work safe and efficient.

2. Deployment & Control

Field teams deploy drones/robots with remote oversight; autonomous navigation handles complex approaches.

3. Data & Cleaning

Capture high-resolution imagery, thermal, LiDAR or perform automated cleaning cycles with PV-safe consumables.

4. Reporting & Action

Deliver annotated reports, 3D models and maintenance recommendations integrating into client workflows.

Krishi-Inspect Platform

Purpose: Automated façade & roof inspection with AI-assisted defect detection.

  • Autonomy: Visual-inertial navigation + RTK positioning
  • Payloads: 50MP RGB, FLIR thermal, optional LiDAR
  • Deliverables: Annotated report, defect heatmap, 3D model
  • Typical use-cases: Glass façade inspections, roof membrane checks, PV string fault localization

Ideal for: Real-estate asset managers, facility teams, O&M contractors.

Krishi-Clean Unit

Purpose: Drone-mounted, PV-safe deep cleaning module for solar arrays and glass façades.

  • Cleaning mode: Soft-brush + deionized water spray + smart drying
  • Efficiency: Up to 300 panels/day per system (depending on layout)
  • Conservation: Low water usage cycles & recyclable fluids
  • Safety: Non-contact/no ladder operations

Ideal for: Solar plant owners, commercial façade maintenance.
